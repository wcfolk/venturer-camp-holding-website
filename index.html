Venturer Camp 2023 took place from 5 to 12 August 2023 at Biblins Youth Campsite, a site managed by Woodcraft Folk. We engaged 300 young people aged 13 to 17 for a week of camping taking in the lovely surroundings of the Wye Valley, enjoying workshops and socialising with friends old and new. It was a huge success and paves the way for an inspired and proactive next generation of talent!

You can find out more about Venturer Camp and Woodcraft Folk through the links to our social media below.

Have I mentioned yet that there's another Venturer Camp just around the corner. In 2026, Venturer Camp will be back - engaging even more young people for their Venturer Camp experience.
