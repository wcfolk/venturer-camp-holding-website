venturercamp.org.uk

Venturer Camp 2023 took place from 5 to 12 August 2023 at Biblins Youth Campsite, a site managed by Woodcraft Folk. We engaged 300 young people aged 13 to 17 for a week of camping taking in the lovely surroundings of the Wye Valley, enjoying workshops and socialising with friends old and new.

We attracted lots of press interest throughout the week. Coop News published a review of the event and The Ross Gazette published a piece at the start of the event looking forward to the exciting things we were bringing to the valley!

Have I mentioned yet that there's another Venturer Camp just around the corner. In 2026, Venturer Camp will be back - engaging even more young people for their Venturer Camp experience.
